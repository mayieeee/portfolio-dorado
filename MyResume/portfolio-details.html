Dal-Ani Smart Cooler - Innovation Olympics 3.0

Project Overview

Dal-Ani Smart Cooler is an IoT-enabled refrigeration solution designed to address the critical problem of vegetable spoilage in the Philippines. Our team developed this innovative solution during Innovation Olympics 3.0, a national startup-style hackathon, where we achieved Top 10 placement among hundreds of competing teams.

Problem We Solved

In the Philippines, vegetable spoilage is a significant issue that affects both farmers and consumers. Poor storage conditions, lack of temperature monitoring, and inadequate preservation methods lead to substantial food waste and economic losses. Traditional cooling systems are often expensive, energy-intensive, and lack smart monitoring capabilities.

Our Solution: Smart Cooling Technology

Dal-Ani Smart Cooler combines IoT technology with efficient cooling mechanisms to provide:

  • Smart Temperature Control: Arduino-based monitoring system that maintains optimal storage conditions
  • Mobile App Integration: Flutter-developed app for real-time monitoring and alerts
  • Energy Efficiency: Optimized cooling algorithms to reduce power consumption
  • Spoilage Prevention: Predictive alerts to prevent vegetable deterioration
  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design for easy operation by farmers and vendors
